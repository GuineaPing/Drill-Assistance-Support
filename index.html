Drill Assistant

User Manual

Welcome to Drill Assistant! This application is designed to help you efficiently memorize words, phrases, verses, proverbs, or song lyrics. Whether you are learning a new language or memorizing your favorite quotes, Drill Assistant makes the process engaging and organized with customizable features.

Main Features

1. Organize Text into Lists

You can easily create and manage lists of words or phrases that you want to learn. Each list can contain items that are meaningful to youâ€”whether that's vocabulary for language study, verses for performance, or even lyrics to your favorite songs.

2. Text-to-Speech Playback

Drill Assistant uses a text-to-speech (TTS) engine to help you practice your text aloud. Each word or phrase in your list can be played back in different voices and speeds, enhancing your ability to memorize effectively.

  • Playback Modes: Select from multiple playback modes, including:
    • Single Repeat: Repeat a single word or phrase.
    • List Playback: Play back each item in a list.
    • Reverse List: Practice the list items in reverse order.
    • Random Playback: Mix it up with random selection of words.

3. Add Comments and Translations

To enrich your learning experience, you can add comments or translations for each word or phrase. You can choose to display or hide these comments during playback, depending on your needs.

4. Import and Export Lists

Drill Assistant makes it easy to manage your lists across devices:

  • Import lists from JSON format or plain text files.
  • Export your lists for safekeeping or to share with friends.

WatchOS Companion

Drill Assistant has an Apple Watch companion app that enables you to control playback remotely and provides instant access to your learning material, right on your wrist. This feature is perfect for quick review sessions when you're on the go.

Editing Your Lists

The Words List Editor allows you to:

  • Edit your texts by adding or modifying items.
  • Assign different languages and voices to specific words or phrases, giving you the flexibility to choose the most effective learning approach.

Settings Overview

You can customize the playback settings to fit your learning style:

  • Playback Mode: Choose between different repeat settings (single word, whole list, random, etc.).
  • Voice Rate: Adjust the speed of the TTS playback to suit your comfort level.
  • Voice Pitch: Customize the pitch of the playback voice to make the practice session more pleasant.
  • Playback Repeats: Set how many times an item should be repeated.
  • Pause Durations: Set pauses between items or before playing comments.
  • Show or Hide Comments: Control whether the additional notes or translations are shown during playback.

Getting Started

  1. Create Your List: Start by creating a new list of words or phrases that you want to learn.
  2. Customize Playback Settings: Use the settings menu to adjust playback preferences like rate, pitch, and mode.
  3. Play Back Your Lists: Hit the Read button to begin listening and memorizing. You can also control playback using your Apple Watch for convenience.
  4. Edit or Import Lists: Add new words or import an entire list from your files.

Apple Watch Quick Control

With the Apple Watch companion app, you can:

  • Play back your current list with the Read button.
  • Switch between lists effortlessly.
  • Adjust playback settings like rate and playback mode directly from your watch, making it ideal for spontaneous review sessions.

Tips for Effective Use

Repetition Is Key: Use the playback repeat options to drill words into memory.

Experiment with Settings: Adjusting pitch and speed can make the words easier to remember by adding variation to your practice.

Remote Practice: Use the watch companion to study while multitasking, like during a walk or workout.
